I’M MARIA JOSÉ GALEANO, THE PERSON BEHIND THE SCREEN

Hi there! I’m passionate about designing products that make life simpler and more enjoyable for people. I mean, being human is already complex enough, right? Why not make it better?

My journey started in UX/UI Design, but I discovered my true calling in the combination of Product Design and Data. That’s why I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Management with a focus on Analytics at the University of Palermo.

Since 2022, I’ve been formally engaged in digital product design, gaining valuable experience in building mobile applications, SaaS platforms, and B2B system software. This journey has given me a profound understanding of the core principles of digital design and their real-world impact on users.

A Glimpse into My Journey

I’m originally from Paraguay and currently reside in Torino, Italy. At 17, I embarked on a life-changing journey through a cultural exchange program in Germany. Moving from my small hometown of 30,000 people, this experience exposed me to a world full of diverse cultures, languages, and learnings, sparking not just a journey across continents but also one of personal growth.

Living amidst such diversity, I learned to view challenges as opportunities for growth—for myself and for the communities I engage with. This mindset has been a guiding force in my professional life, constantly seeking to turn obstacles into avenues for innovation and positive change. I’m excited to bring this perspective to your team, contributing not just my technical skills but also a global and growth-oriented approach.

If my journey resonates with you or if you’re curious about how we might collaborate, I’d love to hear from you ✨!

Feel free to reach out with any thoughts, questions, or opportunities. Let’s start a conversation and explore the possibilities together!
